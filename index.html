Read with friends

At Silent Book Club® there's no assigned reading. SBC members gather in bars, cafés, bookstores, libraries, and online to read together in quiet camaraderie.

All readers are welcome—ebooks or audiobooks, poetry or prose, fiction or non-fiction... it's BYOBook.

FIND ONE NEAR YOU

Silent Book Club is a global community of readers that brings book lovers together for quiet reading and connection. With more than 1,000 events held across 50 countries, each gathering is organized and led by dedicated local volunteers. These events offer a welcoming, stress-free environment where participants can enjoy reading at their own pace, without the pressure of assigned books or discussions, fostering a shared love of literature and community.
